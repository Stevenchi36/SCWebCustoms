How it works

Step 1: Communicate

Send your ideas about how you want your site to look, including images, text, and colors!

Step 2: Draft

I will create a draft of the site using your ideas! This could take anywhere from a day to a few weeks depending on the complexity.

Step 3: Review

I will send you the draft and you can review it and tell me what changes you would like to be made.

Step 4: Final Product

Final changes will be made and I can send you the website, or put it up myself!
